Introducing the Santa Cruz Tallboy 4, the ultimate mountain bike for riders who demand the best. With a choice of carbon fiber or aluminum frame, this bike is built to handle any terrain with ease. The VPP suspension design provides 120mm of travel, while the Fox 34 or 36 fork with 130mm of travel and Fox Float Performance DPS or DPX2 rear shock ensure a smooth ride. The 29-inch wheels are fitted with Maxxis Minion DHR II (rear) and Maxxis Minion DHF (front) tires for maximum traction and control. 

Choose from Shimano or SRAM hydraulic disc brakes and drivetrains, and a Santa Cruz carbon or aluminum handlebar and RaceFace or Santa Cruz stem. The RockShox Reverb or Fox Transfer dropper seatpost and WTB or Santa Cruz saddle provide comfort and control on even the toughest trails. 

With a weight of just 2.7 kg (6 lbs) for carbon fiber frame models and 3.4 kg (7.5 lbs) for aluminum frame models (without pedals), this bike is lightweight and easy to handle. Other features include boost spacing on front and rear hubs for increased wheel stiffness and strength, threaded bottom bracket for easy maintenance, internal cable routing for a clean look and protection from the elements, and compatibility with 1x or 2x drivetrains. 

Available in multiple colors and build kits to suit different riding styles and preferences, the Santa Cruz Tallboy 4 is the perfect choice for serious mountain bikers who demand the best.
